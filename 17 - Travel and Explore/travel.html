Frequently Asked Questions

Airport Hotel Transportation?

There are a variety of options to get to your vacation home from the Cancun airport. The estimated time of the journey is 45 minutes to 1 hour, depending on the means of transport you choose. You should also consider the time it will take you to leave the airport terminal, collect your luggage and go through migration.

  • Bus: The Mexican company of Land Transport ADO offers service from airport terminals 2, 3 and 4 towards Playa del Carmen. They are located at the exit of the terminals and have ample space for your bags. It is likely that as part of the route you will have to go to the other terminals and disembark at both the 30 Av terminal and the Av. Juárez terminal in Playa del Carmen.
  • Private Transport: We offer the option of Private Transportation service in Vans with prices from. In order to schedule your service contact your Maya Ocean Real Estate or Vacation Advisor and share your flight details. One of the most important advantages of the private service is knowing that the driver will always be waiting for you despite delays or flight changes and of course the comfort offered by the large transport units.
  • Rent A Car: Inside the Cancun International Airport are some of the world’s most recognized car rental companies with vehicle delivery and reception services. They offer car rental with prices from 380 MXN per day . If your intention is to explore the Riviera Maya or if you come with your family, renting a vehicle may be the best option.
  • Courtesy Transportation During The Property Visit: Relax and enjoy the property tour. We take care of going to pick you up where you are staying and take you to know the Properties and investments that are of your interest accompanied by one of our Real Estate Advisors who will guide you regarding the different residential areas, their different, market trends and options for housing and investment according to your needs.
